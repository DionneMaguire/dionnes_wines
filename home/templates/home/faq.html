Delivery, Returns & Frequently Asked Questions

Delivery

Deliveries usualyy take 2-3 working days but during busy periods such as Christmas and Black friday deliveries may take up to 5 working days.

Returns

If you wish to return or exchange please contact dionneswines@example.com.

In the unfortunate case of goods being faulty or damaged in transit, O’Briens offer the choice of either a replacement or a full refund including any postage costs incurred. Please contact dionneswines@example.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum order amount to shop online?

There is no minimum order when shopping with us online. Delivery is free if the order exceeds €60.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order immediately up to the point of dispatch.
